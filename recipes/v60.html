Filtered V60

The Hario staple

This is probably the first step in the world of specialty coffee. And is really easy make some delicious coffee.

Equipments

  • V60 dripper
  • V60 filter
  • Scale at least to .1 of a gram
  • Water boiling device
  • Some receptacle for the coffee

Ingredients

  • Freshly roasted coffee beans

Steps

  1. Weight 7.5g of coffee grounds per 100g of water
  2. Boil the water
  3. Rinse the paper filter mounted on the dripper with the boiled water
  4. Put the grounded coffee on the dripper (and the set on the receptacle
  5. Start pouring over:
    1. Blooming: pour double the weight of the grounded coffee and wait 30-45 seconds
    2. First pour: half of the desired final volume
    3. The rest of the volume: divide in three equals pours
  6. Drink the coffee: it seems obvious but it is REALLY important. Make every adjustment in your process based on taste, not on anything else
