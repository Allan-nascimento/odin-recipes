The Lasagna

This is a quite simple lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Ricotta
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Make the sauce: with ground beef, bell peppers, onions, and a combo of tomato sauce, tomato paste, and crushed tomatoes.
  2. Boil the noodles
  3. Prepare the cheeses: just shred and mix if you want
  4. Assembly: a cup of meat sauce, followed by a layer of noodles, more sauce, followed by a layer of cheese. Repeat until you get three layers.
  5. Bake until get to see bubbles
