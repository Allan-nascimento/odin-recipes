Espresso: picopresso

The Ultra-Portable Espresso

The Picopresso is the a really capable manual espresso machine, but requires some good skill in order to make use of it's full potential.

Equipments

  • Picopresso machine
  • A Good, espresso capable grinder
  • Scale at least to .1 of a gram
  • Water boiling device
  • Some receptacle for the coffee

Ingredients

  • Freshly roasted coffee beans

Steps

  1. Weight 17-19g of coffee grounds
  2. Grind the grounds within espresso range
  3. Boil the water
  4. Pre heat the machine
  5. Distribute and tamp the coffee on the basket and porta-filter
  6. Brew the coffee:
    1. Pump 8-10 times for the pre-infusion
    2. Wait about 10 seconds, it will start dripping
    3. Restart pumping at a steady, but not rushed, pace, always weighting the output until the desired weight ratio
  7. Drink the coffee: it seems obvious but it is REALLY important. Make every adjustment in your process based on taste, not on anything else
